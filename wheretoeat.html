Location: Poblacion Norte, Batuan, Bohol
Accommodations: Catering, Food Deliveries, Reservations, Walk-in Dining
Specialties: Pizza, Filipino Cuisine, Western and Asian Fusion Cuisines


            Basak Bistro has become an instant local sensation for bringing the big city flavors to the countryside at a very reasonable price.
            The name was taken from the word “Basak” which means “Rice Fields” or “Rice Farms” where the bistro was exactly located. It is a combination of a classic city lifestyle and a local vicinity ambiance.
            Who can really find a taste-class bistro in the middle of the mountain? Preferably in the middle of the hills? This bistro opens daily around 12:00 noon to 8:00pm. They also plays live music and singers while you eat. This roadside bistro has a convenient location, if you are heading to Tagbilaran, just take a short turn from the intersection from the Public Market of Batuan and you can see the huge sign board of it, but if you are heading to Balilihan, it’s perfect, it is just located exactly beside the highway.
            Basak Bistro is a family owned business. They are offering various menu that is rightly perfect for your taste. From pizzas to carbonaras, from potato fries to shakes and halo-halo, from cakes to muffins and most of all from smoothies to coffees. They are offering everything that you can find in the city. With their own tag line that says, “Big City Flavor, Small Town Rate!.” you can’t go wrong with them.

Location: Poblacion Norte, Batuan, Bohol
Accommodations: Catering, Food Deliveries, Reservations, Walk-in Dining
Specialties: Ice Cream, Local Filipino Cuisines


            Turk’z Ice Cream began as a “dirty ice cream” parlor which garnered a massive crowd for their affordable and delicious flavoured ice creams. Now, they are expanding their business into the culinary industry.
            They just built their native resto-hut just alongside the main highway which offers morning coffee and breakfast for joggers and bikers. They also started offering walk-in dining and reservations for people who loves to get a taste of authentic local cuisines.
            Of course, the experience would be incomplete without their locally-renowned ice cream!

            Twin’s BBQ Grill has always been a favorite for students, municipal employees and passersby all just the same. Its location, which is within the town square, makes it ideal for people who are in a rush but still wants to get good food.
            The resto offers local cuisines and barbecue either for dine-in, take-out or reservations. Aside from their delicious menu, they also offer food packages for different occasions including homemade cakes and other pastries.

Location: Poblacion Norte, Batuan, Bohol
Accommodations: Catering, Food Deliveries, Walk-in Dining
Specialties: Local Filipino Cuisines, Various Snacks


            If a busy setting is your dining aesthetic then Six R’s Eatery is the restaurant for you.
            Named after the owner’s six children whose names begin with the letter “R”, Six R’s Eatery is a favorite among shoppers and travelers due to it being located in the marketplace. The restaurant offers local cuisines that has satisfied many Batuananons and tourists alike for years now.
            The restaurant also provides karaoke sessions for people who just wants to have a good time with their friends and family.
